Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

Dr. Kalam — The Missile Man of India and former President who inspired millions with his vision and simplicity.

Dr. Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam was an Indian aerospace scientist and the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007. He played a crucial role in India's civilian space and military missile development programs. He was also a beloved teacher and author, inspiring students across the country with his motivational speeches and books.

His contributions to India's defense and education systems earned him the title of "People’s President." Even after his presidency, he continued to engage with students and advocate for the power of dreams and knowledge.
